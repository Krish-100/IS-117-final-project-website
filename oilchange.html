Steps for Changing Your Oil

  • List of Tools
  • 1. Oil filter wrench
  • 2. Oil drain pan
  • 3. Jack and jackstands
  • 4.Socket set
  • 5. Funnel
  • 6. New oil & filter
  • Step 1:Preperation and Safety
    Gather tools and materials, including an oil filter wrench, drain pan, socket set, and the correct oil and filter. Warm the engine, lift the car securely, and ensure safety with jack stands. 10 mins
  • Step 2: Drain Old Oil
    Position the drain pan beneath the oil pan, locate and remove the drain plug using a socket wrench, allowing the old oil to fully drain. 10 mins
  • Step3: Replace Oil Filter
    Use an oil filter wrench to remove the old filter. Apply oil to the new filter's gasket, hand-tighten it, and ensure a secure fit 5 mins
  • Step 4: Refill And Dispose
    Insert a funnel, add the recommended oil quantity, and check the level. Dispose of old oil and the filter responsibly at a recycling center or designated facility. 5 mins
