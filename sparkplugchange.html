Steps for Changing Your Spark Plugs

  • List of Tools
  • 1. Spark plug socket
  • 2. Ratchet set
  • 3. Socket entension
  • 4. Torque wrench
  • 5. Dielectric grease
  • 6. Spark plug boot puller
  • 7. Compresser air
  • Step 1:Preperation and Safety
    Wear appropriate gear, and make sure the engine is cool. 3 mins
  • Step 2: Locate Spark Plugs
    Refer to your vehicle manual for their position. 2 mins
  • Step3: Remove Wires and Coils
    Carefully disconnect spark plug wires or ignition coils 10 mins
  • Step 4: Extract Old Spark Plugs
    Use the spark plug socket to loosen and remove each spark plug. 10 mins
  • Step 5: Check and Adjust Gap
    Use the gap tool to check and adjust the gap of the new spark plugs as per your vehicle specifications. 10 mins
  • Step 6: Install New Spark Plugs
    Hand-thread the new plugs and then use the socket and ratchet to tighten to the recommended torque. 10 mins
  • Step 7: Reconnect Wires and Coils
    Attach spark plug wires or ignition coils. Optionally, use dielectric grease on the boots for better connections. 10 mins
  • Step 8: Test and Check
    Start the engine, ensuring smooth operation and checking for any misfires or issues. Verify all connections and perform a final check. 5 mins
